We extracted three different CSV’s from the website Data.world to find a correlation between airports, airlines and routes in the United States.

We imported the CSV’s to Jupyter lab and transform it into three working dataframes by cleaning, filtering, dropping null cells and connecting duplicates like the the IATA and the ICAO code that are unique for each airport.

Another relationship with the airport table could be established through the airport_id field and the airline_id field to make the connection with the routes table needed to make the connection into Postgres database

after that, all 3 tables were joined into 1 large table that visualizes the correlation in between all three dataframes. Then exported into HTML format to be integrated into the website.

Why we chose this?

Airports are essential for the economic development of any place. They improve communications, promote job creation, generate economic activity and revalue the environment, so it would be of great importance to organize and analyze their data.
